Scrambled Eggs

Ingredients:
Eggs
Milk
Salt
Pepper

Directions:
1. Add eggs, milk, salt, and pepper to bowl
2. Mix with whisk
3. Pour mixture into hot skillet
4. Cook, stirring occasionally
5. Eat delicious scrambled eggs.
